YAMAHA MT09

The MT-09 is the first Yamaha motorcycle since the XS750 and XS850 to be powered by an inline-three engine. Both are shaft-driven motorcycles produced from 1976 to 1981. In 2017, the MT-09 was updated with fully adjustable suspension, traction control, ABS, slipper clutch, LED headlights and updated styling.

KAWASAKI Z900

Launched in 1972, the 900 Z1 - more commonly known as the Z 900 - has entered motorcycle history to become a mythical machine thanks to its incomparable line, a subtle blend of sobriety and perfect elegance, and its dynamic performance that will outperform the reference Honda 750 Four
